Why Vote.USA.gov?

We wanted to create an amazing experience for voters.

In response to the significant number of visitors on USA.gov that regularly query voter registration, we created a mechanism for registering to vote, connecting people to more information about voting rights and participation, and mobilizing citizens to exercise their right to vote & engage meaningfully in our democracy.
